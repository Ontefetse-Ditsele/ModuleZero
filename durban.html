Durban


Durban, a coastal city in eastern South Africa’s KwaZulu-Natal province, is known for its African,
Indian and colonial influences. Refurbished for soccer’s 2010 World Cup, the seafront promenade runs from uShaka Marine World,
a huge theme park with an aquarium, to the futuristic Moses Mabhida Stadium.
The Durban Botanical Gardens showcases African plant species
